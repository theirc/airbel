What is User-Centred Design?

User-centred design is the process of understanding the underlying drivers of the problem from the user’s perspective, looking for inspiration and opportunities from their stories. We then generate early ideas based on this feedback and pre-test these ideas with users. Their feedback helps us refine those ideas quickly before investing time and resources to implement them at scale.
